# Luminate: Entertainment Industry Data, Analytics, and Audience Insights

- **Source:** Luminate
- **URL:** https://luminatedata.com/music/
- **Author:** Luminate
- **Date:** Accessed 2025

## Content

Luminate is the preeminent entertainment data and insights company, providing access to objective and verified information across music, film, and television. The platform compiles data from over 500 verified streaming, retail, and airplay sources to analyze millions of artists, albums, and songs, making it the most comprehensive data source in the entertainment industry. Luminate powers the Billboard Charts, described as the most influential charts in the music industry, establishing it as the authoritative data reference point for the entire entertainment sector.

A critical differentiator for Luminate is its data quality methodology. Unlike competitors, Luminate's data is never scraped, estimated, or crowdsourced. All information undergoes verification and validation by in-house data science teams, ensuring accuracy and reliability for business-critical decisions. The platform provides artist, album, and song-level dashboards with country-level consumption metrics across 60 international markets, market share tracking, and analysis modules for deeper insights.

For audience segmentation, Luminate Intelligence reports provide detailed profiles of specific audiences, demographics, and global markets. These reports cover genre fanbases, artist fans, demographic segments, and country-specific audiences, enabling entertainment companies to understand not just what people are listening to but who those listeners are and how to reach them. This segmentation capability is essential for concert promoters, marketers, and booking agents who need to match artists with the right audiences in the right markets.

Luminate recently acquired Quansic, which maintains the world's largest artist identification database containing over 2.5 million artist identifiers (ISNIs) and 200 million asset identifiers. This acquisition strengthens Luminate's ability to accurately track and attribute music consumption across platforms, resolving a longstanding challenge in music data where the same artist or song might appear under different identifiers on different services.

The platform integrates with major entertainment companies including Universal Music Group, Sony Music, Warner Music Group, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, Live Nation, and Disney Music Group, along with independent labels like BMG. This broad integration base means that Luminate's audience segmentation and consumption data spans the full spectrum of the industry, making it uniquely positioned to provide insights that bridge the gap between recorded music consumption and live entertainment attendance patterns.
