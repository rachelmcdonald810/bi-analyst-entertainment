# AEG's AXS Ticketing Technology and Data Strategy

- **Source:** AEG Worldwide
- **URL:** https://aegworldwide.com/divisions/ticketing-other-businesses
- **Author:** AEG Worldwide
- **Date:** Accessed 2025

## Content

AXS is a global ticketing platform founded in 2011 as a joint venture led by Anschutz Entertainment Group (AEG), providing ticketing, marketing, data analytics, and fan engagement solutions for the live entertainment industry. AEG purchased all outstanding shares of AXS in September 2019, bringing its ownership to 100% and positioning the platform as a fully integrated part of its entertainment ecosystem.

Through its full-event lifecycle solution set, AXS manages every aspect of the ticketing, in-event, and post-event experience. A key differentiator is AXS's ability to provide clients with fully trackable data as tickets pass from primary to secondary markets and from one fan to the next. This end-to-end tracking gives venues and promoters visibility into the complete ticket journey, enabling better analytics and fan relationship management.

AXS serves over 1,300 major entertainment and sports brands globally across more than 500 premier venues, sports teams, and event organizers in North America, Europe, Asia, Australia, and New Zealand. Notable clients include the LA28 Olympic and Paralympic Games, Coachella Music and Arts Festival, Red Rocks Amphitheatre, The O2, and various professional sports organizations.

The platform's technology capabilities include proprietary AXS Mobile ID technology for secure digital ticketing, ID-based ticketing for fan identification, dynamic pricing tools, and integrated data analytics. Through a partnership with Lawson Entertainment in Japan, AXS expanded these capabilities to the Japanese market, providing mobile services, dynamic pricing, and data analytics for Japan's B.League and other events.

AEG uses Twilio Segment as a customer data platform across its audience touchpoints, linking profiling and analytics around fan preferences before, during, and after events. This customer data platform strategy enables AEG to build comprehensive fan profiles that inform marketing decisions, pricing strategies, and personalized experiences across its entire portfolio of venues and events.

AXS Europe powers both primary and resale marketplaces, leveraging integrated technology and analytics to enable clients to sell the right ticket to the right fan at the right price. The platform also acquired WRSTBND, an event technology company, to expand its capabilities in wearable technology and contactless experiences at live events.
